Project name : Manaforesta
Project launched year : 2019
About:
Mana Foresta is a 14 storey Vertical Forest Tower, with homes covered in greenery and presenting a forest-like environment, that brings together experts in botany, sustainability and ecology to create a radical project that revolutionizes the concept of living space. This is a residential tower not covered in metal, but in leaves, shrubs, trees and creepers. This is a perfect home for people looking to stay away from the hustle of crowded city life. A perfect home with plenty of trees and shrubs around, you can enjoy the company of nature at its greenest form here in these houses. The best part of it is that you do not have to travel away from the city as these homes are located well within Bengaluru city.
